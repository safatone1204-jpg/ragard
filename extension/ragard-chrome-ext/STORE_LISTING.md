## Detailed Description

```
Ragard Sidebar Extension - Real-Time Content Analysis

Analyze posts and financial articles in real-time while you scroll. Get instant AI-powered insights on stock tickers, sentiment, and market context without leaving the page.

KEY FEATURES:
• Real-Time Analysis - Analyze content as you browse, perfect for scrolling through Reddit, financial articles, and news
• Automatic Stock Ticker Detection - Identifies stock symbols mentioned in posts and articles
• AI-Powered Sentiment Analysis - Understand the sentiment and context of discussions instantly
• Side Panel Interface - Convenient sidebar that stays open while you scroll through content
• Multi-Source Support - Works with Reddit, financial articles, Twitter/X, Stocktwits, and more
• Works Best with Reddit - Optimized for Reddit posts but great for financial articles too
• Auto-Detection - Automatically works with both production and local development environments

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Navigate to any post or financial article (works best on Reddit, e.g., r/wallstreetbets, r/stocks)
2. Open the Ragard sidebar - it stays open while you scroll
3. Click to analyze any post or article in real-time
4. View instant insights on stock tickers, sentiment, and content context without leaving the page

PERFECT FOR:
• Stock traders analyzing market discussions while scrolling through Reddit or financial news
• Investors who want real-time insights as they browse content
• Researchers studying market sentiment from social media and articles
• Anyone wanting instant analysis without interrupting their browsing flow
• Financial analysts tracking stock mentions and sentiment across sources

PRIVACY & SECURITY:
Your data is secure. The extension only analyzes content you explicitly request analysis for. All analysis happens through our secure API. See our privacy policy at ragardai.com/privacy

Visit ragardai.com for more features, analytics, and market insights.
```

## Privacy Practices

### Single Purpose
**No** - The extension serves multiple purposes (analyzing posts and articles, detecting stock tickers, sentiment analysis)

### Host Permissions
**Yes** - Required to analyze content on various websites. The extension needs access to Reddit.com, financial news sites, and other websites where users want to analyze content.

**Explanation for reviewers:**
"The extension requires host permissions to analyze posts, financial articles, and other content to extract stock ticker information and sentiment. It only processes content when explicitly requested by the user through the extension interface."

### User Data
**Yes** - The extension stores user preferences locally (auto-detect settings, manual API/URL configuration). No personal data is collected or transmitted.

**Privacy Policy URL**: https://ragardai.com/privacy

## Permissions Justification

When asked to justify permissions, use these explanations:

- **activeTab**: Required to access and analyze the content of the current page (Reddit, articles, etc.)
- **scripting**: Required to inject analysis scripts into pages to extract content
- **storage**: Stores user preferences (auto-detect settings, manual API URLs) locally
- **tabs**: Detects which browser tab is active to determine environment (production vs local)
- **sidePanel**: Displays analysis results in Chrome's side panel interface
- **optional_host_permissions (http/https)**: Required to work on Reddit.com, financial news sites, and other websites where users browse content
